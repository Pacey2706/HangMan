Player Instructions:
1) The program will present you with how many 
guesses are left.
2) The hidden word is covered by "*"
3) You will have a input feild where you may choose 
your guess by typing a letter

6) if all guesses have been used up you will lose the game 
7) if you guess the word correctly you will recieve a congratulation 
message stating you have won
